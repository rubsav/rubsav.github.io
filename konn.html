KONN


Four broke highschool students yearning for a different life.
A ruthless Detective, investigating a deadly drug-ring linked to a Kingpin mastermind. In the search for love and wealth, the highschoolers find the deal of a lifetime and an opportunity for what they always dreamed of. As paths cross the boys will soon discover that nothing comes without it's consequences.




KONN showcased at the Canadian Tamil Film Artist Association
